Injection Mold Design & Engineering Services

VindyDesign Studio provides end-to-end injection mold design and engineering support focused on manufacturability, reliability, and first-trial success.

Injection Mold Design (3D & 2D CAD)

Complete mold design from concept to manufacturing-ready drawings. Includes parting strategy, core-cavity design, sliders, lifters, and detailed 2D documentation as per global tooling standards.

Mold Flow Analysis & DFM Support

Early-stage mold flow analysis and DFM evaluation to identify potential molding risks before tool cutting. This reduces rework, improves quality, and shortens development cycles.

Complex Side-Action & Multi-Stage Mold Design

Specialized design support for high-complexity molds involving side actions, angled lifters, collapsible cores, and multi-stage mechanisms designed for repeatability and long tool life.

Precision Mold Manufacturing Support

Technical support during mold manufacturing including vendor coordination, design clarifications, trial issue analysis, and tooling optimization.

Reverse Engineering & CAD Conversion

Accurate recreation of components and tooling from physical samples, legacy drawings, or scan data. Delivered as parametric CAD models and production-ready drawings.

Pilot & Production Mold Support

Technical ownership from pilot molds to high-volume production tools with a focus on first-trial success, cycle time optimization, and consistent quality.
