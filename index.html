About Us

VindyDesign Studio delivers precision injection mold design and manufacturing solutions for automotive, electrical, consumer, and industrial plastic components. Our focus is on production-ready tooling that meets global quality standards, backed by strong DFM practices and reliable on-time delivery.

Why VindyDesign Studio

  • 12+ years of hands-on injection mold design experience
  • Technical ownership of 100+ molds from DFM to mold readiness
  • Proven first-trial success on complex and precision molds
  • Expertise in tight-tolerance tooling and global quality standards
  • Trusted by Indian and international customers

International Customer Footprint

We support customers across multiple regions, delivering injection mold design and manufacturing solutions to both Indian and international markets.
